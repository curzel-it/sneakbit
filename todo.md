# Co-op TODO — per-player parity with single-player

Goal: in co-op (offline split-screen **and** online), every player gets the same
self-contained experience they'd have in single player. No shared/global state for
inventory or wallet — each player owns their own.

- [ ] **1. Per-player inventory** — each player has its own dedicated inventory,
  identical to the inventory they'd have in single player.
- [ ] **2. NPC interaction** — every player can interact with NPCs.
- [ ] **3. Hint interaction** — every player can interact with hints.
- [ ] **4. Shop access** — every player can access the shop: talk to the shop clerk,
  buy items into *their own* inventory, etc.
- [ ] **5. Per-player wallet** — every player has its own wallet (same per-player
  ownership rule as the inventory in #1).
- [ ] **6. Guest starter sword** — guests entering the game with **< 5 kunai** and
  **no melee weapon** in their inventory are automatically gifted a sword.
