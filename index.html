What is it

Explore a handcrafted world

Forests, farmland, dusk-woods and dungeons — a connected world built tile by tile.

Fight & survive

Weapons, monsters, and a demon lord at the end of it. Real combat, not just walking around.

Farm & build

Grow crops, gather, and make a place your own between the fights.

Play together

Drop-in co-op over the network — host a world and bring a friend along.
